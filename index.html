About Julia Frances

Julia Frances qualified as an holistic aromatherapist in 2002 whilst living in Hong Kong. On her return to the UK in 2003, she set up her practice and worked for 12 years building up a very successful business. Over this time she added additional treatments, but then discovered an approach called NO HANDS massage, which predominantly uses the forearms and weight distribution and takes both the therapist’s and clients well-being into consideration.

No Hands Massage

NO HANDS stands for excellence in the quality and power of the touch that you receive. Delivering safe touch through soft yet strong parts of the body, like the soft fleshy part of the forearm, NO HANDS is for people who want more than just a ‘skin rub’.
It is about change, letting go, releasing and transforming ourselves. Sometimes we know in advance what this change is going to be and sometimes we just trust, let go and only realise what it was all about two weeks after the session!
